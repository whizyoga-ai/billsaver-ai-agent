Utility Bill Optimizer for Middle-Class Households

The Problem

Middle-class families overpay on utilities (electricity, gas, water) due to complexity: time-of-use rates, tiered pricing, seasonal plans. Billions in rebates go unclaimed (IRA weatherization, state efficiency programs). Most households lack time/expertise to optimize bills.

The Solution

BillSaver AI deploys four specialized AI agents working autonomously:

  • Rate Plan Analyzer: Compares all provider options
  • Usage Pattern Detector: Identifies waste & anomalies
  • Rebate Hunter: Surfaces all applicable rebates/credits
  • Negotiation Agent: Auto-contacts providers for better rates

Technology Stack: GPT-4/LangChain AI agents, utility API integrations (Green Button, UtilityAPI), smart meter data parsing. Bank-level security, OAuth connections.
